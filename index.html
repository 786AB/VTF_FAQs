FAQs

Category 1: Assignment Related Questions.

1. Due dates of assignments?

Ans. Assignments are due on Wednesday at 11:59 pm PST. Due dates are also visible in your local time zone in google classroom per assignment. Optionally, you can click on google calendar to see assignment due dates from the classroom view.

2. What is the Weekly Certificate of Completion? What if unable to receive it on time?

Ans. It is UNGRADED and OPTIONAL assignment. It is for interns to claim a certificate for weekly efforts. You can share Certificates of Completion on social media. If you did not fill it and receive a Certificate of Completion before due date, there is no late processing for this.

3. Can I turn in an assignment late?

Ans. No. We will not accept late assignments this term.

4. Unable to submit VT assignments because school, person and any other reason?

Ans. According to VT internship policies, every intern must complete assignments on time or will be disqualified. However, it is an at-will internship which means you can gracefully leave. You are welcome to apply for future internships. There are 10 assignments valued at 100 points each. You must receive a minimum of 701 points to pass the internship and receive your certificate. Therefore, if you miss more than 2 assignments, there is no way you will be able to complete the internship.

5. Are weekly LIVE meetings mandatory?

Ans. No. LIVE meetings are another way of intern collaboration and getting your questions or doubts answered by support staff.

6. How can I get an extension on an assignment?

Ans. Refer to Q3 and Q4.

Category 3: Google Class Related Questions.

9. What does assignment being “returned” mean?

Ans. After grading your assignment, proctors return it for you to have a confirmation that your assignment has been reviewed and graded.

10. How to submit an assignment?

Ans. Each assignment has a complete set of instructions describing how to turn in your assignment for grading.

11. If I am removed from the internship will it affect me if I apply again for future internships?

Ans. It is an at-will internship which means you can gracefully leave by creating a help desk ticket. You can always apply for future internships.
